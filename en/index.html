Features

Everything you need to read your scans

A complete tool to view and analyze DICOM images from your X-rays, CT scans and MRIs.

Zoom & Navigation

Zoom in, zoom out, and navigate images with precision. Browse through multiple scans using the thumbnail sidebar.

Brightness & Contrast

Adjust Window/Level to highlight different tissues. Automatic presets for chest, bone, abdomen, brain and more.

Measurements

Ruler for distances, protractor for angles and ROI for area statistics. Measure directly on the images.

Reports Included

If the CD contains text reports, they are displayed automatically alongside the corresponding images.

Export PNG & PDF

Save images as high-quality PNG or export a complete PDF with all scans and patient data.

Drag & Drop

Drag your DICOM files or folder directly into the window. Also supports file manager selection.

Your data stays yours. Always.

leggiraggi.it is an open-source project: the code is public and verifiable by anyone. It runs entirely in your browser. Your medical images are never uploaded to any server. We collect no data, use no cookies, track nothing.

How it works

Three simple steps

No installation needed. Just a modern browser.

1

Insert the CD

Insert the X-ray CD you received from the hospital into your computer.

2

Open the Viewer

Click "Open Viewer" and drag your DICOM files into the window, or select them from the file manager.

3

View & Explore

Navigate through images, adjust brightness and contrast, take measurements and export to PDF.
